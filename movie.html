The Social Network

Age rating: 18+

Country: USA

Year: 2010

Genre: drama

Director: David Fincher

Actors: Runi Mara,Joseph Mazzello,Rashida Jones, Males Lansford, Jess Eisenberg, Andrew Garfield, Justin Timberlake.

Description: The film tells the story of the creation of one of the most popular social networks on the Internet - Facebook. The resounding success of this network among users around the world forever changed the lives of fellow students at Harvard University, who founded it in 2004 and became the youngest multimillionaires in the United States within a few years.
